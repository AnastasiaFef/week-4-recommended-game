Puppy collector!


You will be given a ramdom number at the start of the game.

There are four puppies below. By petting (clicking on) puppy you will add a specific amount of cuteness to your total satisfaction score.

You win the game by matching you total satisfaction score to random number, you explode if your total score goes above the random number.

The cuteness of each puppy is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the cuteness values of each puppy is changed.
